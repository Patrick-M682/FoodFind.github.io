Grocery List

Updates automatically based on the food in your fridge.

Our grocery list feature uses state of the art sensors in your fridge to automatically update the items in your grocery list based on your needs. Items are colour coded based on urgency.

Deliveries

Our delivery service is designed to provide our customers with a convenient and fast experience.

Our Delivery Feature ensures fast and convenient service, allowing users to order groceries directly through our app. With real-time tracking, you can monitor your delivery from preparation to doorstep. Additionally, users can schedule deliveries at their convenience, ensuring fresh and timely arrivals. This feature aims to save time and enhance your food experience by making high-quality products accessible with just a few clicks.

Recipes

Our recipes feature offers a delightful collection of dishes that inspire creativity in the kitchen.

The FoodFind app offers personalized recipe suggestions based on the ingredients you already have in your fridge. You can tailor this feature to include your preferred meals and dietary restrictions, ensuring that every recipe fits your tastes and nutritional needs. Whether you're looking to create something new or stick to your favorites, FoodFind makes meal planning easy and convenient, helping you reduce food waste while enjoying tasty meals.
